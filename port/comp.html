Complexity Mining and Network Theory

Complementary to our applied social informatics and (Web) systems research, WaCS researchers also develop computational approaches for complex systems and network mining. It includes devising theoretically sound ways to extract network representations from novel data sources, and deriving new measures, metrics, and models useful for mining patterns from them.
