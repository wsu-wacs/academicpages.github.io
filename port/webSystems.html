(Web) Systems, Measurements, and Modeling

The performability, reliability, and security of physical devices that support complex (Web) systems are affected by countless environmental factors. We consider the statistical analysis of "traffic" to a system and behavioral models of "users" to assess the impact of such environmental effects. In light of the emerging Internet of Things concept, we take special interest in understanding and mitigating the impact of Web Robot or Web Crawler traffic on the performability of Web systems and on the security of the information it hosts.
