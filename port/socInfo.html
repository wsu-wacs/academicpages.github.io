Social Informatics

Systems and platforms that give people an opportunity, at scale, to connect, share, and interact with one another are inherently complex. Our social informatics research concerns: (i) theoretical work towards operationalizing long-standing social theories for large-scale communities where interactions are moderated by technology; (ii) the measurement and study of emerging social systems; (iii) devising new measures, metrics, and predictive models that provide consieration for social and interaction factors; (iv) creating systems and platforms built on social media data and analytics.
